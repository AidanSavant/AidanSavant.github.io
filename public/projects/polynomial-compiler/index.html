Introduction

Simple compiler for polynomial expressions and logical expressions.

  • (1 + 5) * 2 / 12
  • ((1 + 5) + 2) + ~(5 + 15)
  • (1 & 2 | 10 + 5 * 2) + ~(1 | 0 & 5)
  • … etc

it also supports error diagnostics at the lexer and parser phases, so it can detect ill-formed expressions, e.g:

  • (1 + 5
  • ++ 5
  • 1 +
  • )(1
  • … etc

and invalid characters, e.g:

  • 1^
  • \1
  • a = 5 (soon to be supported :P)
